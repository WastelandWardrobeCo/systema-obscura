Zone 13

Zone 13 is a contained human ecosystem under pressure. Survival is not random here. It is engineered, traded, enforced, harvested, and sometimes erased.

Canonized Factions

Hemoglyphs

Survival Is Engineered

The Hemoglyphs are Zone 13’s chemists, medics, and compound engineers. Using blood-based chemistry, they create cures, poisons, explosives, synthetic blood, and even drinkable water from paired dry compounds. They are practical, feared, and functionally independent from the system around them.

Cloak & Coin

Everything Has A Price

Cloak & Coin are the black-market authority of Zone 13. They trade in anything that holds value: goods, favors, access, information, body parts, and leverage. Their one unbroken rule is simple: they never lose a transaction. If someone is desperate enough to find them, the cost has already begun.

Harvesters

Nothing Is Wasted

The Harvesters are Zone 13’s extraction and conversion faction. They reclaim components from machines, salvage biological material from the dead, and control the electrical infrastructure by rebuilding systems from stripped parts. If something exists, they see it as usable. If something stops functioning, it becomes theirs.

Axiom

Every Action Has Consequence

The Axiom are the enforcement authority of Zone 13. They do not negotiate, posture, or improvise. They arrive when consequence has already been decided and deliver punishment with exact proportion. Not cruelty, not chaos, not mercy. Finality.

Null

No Record Exists

The Null are not understood so much as noticed by their absence. When the Null touches a person, object, or event, it no longer belongs to the system. No record remains. No transaction survives. No recovery is possible. They are the fracture in Zone 13 that every other faction fails to explain.
